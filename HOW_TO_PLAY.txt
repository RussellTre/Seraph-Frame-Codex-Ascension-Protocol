SERAPH FRAME: ASCENSION PROTOCOL
Quick Start

1. Extract this zip file to a folder.
2. Double-click index.html.
3. Your web browser will open the app.
4. Choose a Team Echo pilot, an opponent division and rival, and an arena.
   Select Custom Future Opponent to enter a new rival and sync percentage.
5. Click Begin Seraph Frame Pilot Engagement and roll initiative.

No installation or internet connection is required.

Controls

Damage Levels

- Every pilot has a 10-point damage track.
- Damage 1-3: Minor strain; defensive reaction rolls suffer -1.
- Damage 4-6: Major damage; all rolls suffer -1.
- Damage 7-9: Near-critical; all rolls suffer -2 and Phantasm/Resonance is unavailable.
- Damage 10: The Frame is destroyed or disabled.

Initiative and Sync

- Initiative rolls gain +1 for each started 20% of the pilot's sync level.
- Examples: 70% Sync grants +4; 85% Sync grants +5; 100% Sync grants +5.
- Seraph Authority pilots operate at 110% Sync and grant +6 initiative.
- In paired or team fights, each active pilot rolls initiative with their own
  sync bonus; the highest modified result leads the formation.

Pilot Duel

- Strike: Attack the opponent with an opposed d20 roll, consuming any empowered
  strike bonus from a successful Maneuver.
- Maneuver: On a clean success of 15 or higher, the next Strike gains +3 on
  its attack roll and deals 2 damage if it hits.
- Guard: On a mixed result, the next defensive reaction gains +3; on a clean
  success, the next defensive reaction gains +5.
- Evasive Vector: On a total of 13 or higher, the next incoming Strike attack
  roll targeting the pilot suffers -3.
- Weapon Specialty: Once per battle, make an enhanced Strike with the pilot's
  named weapon at +2 attack and 2 damage on a hit. It can consume Maneuver setup.
- Phantasm: Activate your once-per-battle divine ability.

2 on 2

- Select 2 on 2 at the top of the simulation screen.
- Select two different Team Echo pilots and two different opponents from one team.
- Use Formation Strike, Tactical Shift, Aegis Cover, Evasive Vector, Weapon
  Specialty, and Resonance as in Team Battle.
- Win by disabling both opposing Frames before both Echo Frames are disabled.

Team Battle

- Select Team Battle at the top of the simulation screen.
- Deploy Team Echo against a rival squad or the Seraph Authority.
- Choose an acting Echo pilot and target each round.
- Formation Strike: Direct attack by the selected pilot.
- Tactical Shift: Grants the acting pilot +3 on their next Formation Strike roll.
- Aegis Cover: Roll a total of 13 or higher to grant that pilot +5 on their
  next defensive reaction against an incoming Formation Strike.
- Evasive Vector: Roll a total of 13 or higher to impose -3 on the next
  incoming Formation Strike attack roll targeting that pilot.
- Weapon Specialty: Once per pilot, make an enhanced Formation Strike using
  that pilot's named weapon at +2 attack and 2 damage on a hit. It can consume
  Tactical Shift setup and prepared defenses apply against it.
- Resonance: Use the selected Echo pilot's once-per-battle Phantasm.
- Win by disabling every Frame in the opposing force.

Dice Outcomes

- 1-7: Failure with consequence.
- 8-14: Mixed result with a drawback.
- 15-20: Clean success.

Goal

Reduce the opposing Seraph Frame through ten damage levels before your
own Frame is destroyed, or disable every opponent in paired and team modes.

      Squad Combat Record

        Codex Brief Combat Engagement Rules Expand

        Shared Rules

        d20 Outcomes 1-7 consequence; 8-14 mixed result; 15-20 clean success.

        Sync Initiative Gain +1 initiative per started 20% sync. Authority units at 110% gain +6.

        Damage Track Each pilot has 10 damage: 1-3 minor strain; 4-6 major damage; 7-9 near-critical; 10 disabled.

        Pilot Duel

        One Echo pilot versus one rival. Win by dealing 10 damage first.

        • Strike Opposed attack roll; consumes prepared Maneuver power.
        • Maneuver On 15+, next Strike gains +3 attack and +1 damage.
        • Guard On 8-14 gain +3 defense; on 15+ gain +5 defense.
        • Evasive Vector On 13+, the next incoming Strike is made at -3.
        • Weapon Specialty Once per battle; make an enhanced Strike with the pilot's weapon at +2 and deal 2 damage on a hit. It can use Maneuver setup.
        • Phantasm Once per battle; success inflicts damage.

        2 on 2

        Two Echo pilots versus two rivals from one team. The highest sync-modified roll leads each pair; disable both opposing Frames to win.

        • Formation Strike The acting pilot attacks the selected target.
        • Tactical Shift Grants +3 on that pilot's next Formation Strike.
        • Aegis Cover On 13+, grants +5 on that pilot's next defense.
        • Evasive Vector On 13+, the next Formation Strike aimed at that pilot is made at -3.
        • Weapon Specialty Once per pilot; make an enhanced Formation Strike with their weapon at +2 and deal 2 damage on a hit. It can use Tactical Shift setup.
        • Resonance Once per pilot per battle.

        Team Battle

        Four Echo pilots versus a full opposing formation. The highest sync-modified roll leads each unit; disable the entire rival force to win.

        Use the same actions as 2 on 2. Weapon Specialty and Resonance are each available once per pilot; tactical and defensive bonuses remain until an applicable attack consumes them.
